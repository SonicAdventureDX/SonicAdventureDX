Okay, here we go:

Hatching a Chao
You can hatch Chao by throwing the egg, hugging the egg, or waiting for it to hatch by itself ... your Chao's personality and traits can be affected depending on how you hatch them. Try and experiment on the best way to hatch one... my suggestion is to keep from throwing it.

Animal Rejection
I guess your Chao doesn't like that animal, or it could be mad at you as well. Try giving it some fruit... if it rejects that too, then yes, it is mad at you. Be nice next time.

When is it an Adult?
Chao go through evolutionary stages. They cocoon to signify entering another evolutionary stage. Chao usually evolve twice... here's how it goes: egg > baby > cocoon (evolve) > young chao > cocoon (evolve 2) > adult

afterwards... they either reincarnate or pass away. This is all depending on how you treat it.

Friends
When you do a Chao VMU Battle or VMU Marriage, your owner information is shared between a chao and it's new friend.... it will now know who your friend is!

Chao Daycare: Purpose
The Chao Daycare is available for you to be able to share your Chao with other Chao Trainers and Breeders all over the world!
