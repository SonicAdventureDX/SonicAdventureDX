SONIC WORLD RANKINGS: OFFICIAL RULES

1. ELIGIBILITY: Anyone in the world with a Sega Dreamcast console, Sonic Adventure or Sonic Adventure International GD-ROM, Internet Access and a valid email address may enter this contest. Children under the age of 13 must have their parent's permission to enter. Employees of Sega of America, Inc. ("Sega") and their immediate family members and Sega's respective affiliates and parents are not eligible. This contest is void where prohibited.
2. PRIZES STRUCTURE: For each individual World Rankings contest:

First Place
First Place trophy
Sonic Champion T-shirt
5 World Rankings Points

Second Place
Second Place trophy
Sonic Champion T-shirt
4 World Rankings Points

Third Place
3rd Place trophy
Sonic Champion T-shirt
3 World Rankings Points

Fourth to Tenth Place
Sonic Champion T-shirt
1 World Rankings Point

T-shirts winners only eligible to win (1) T-shirt. T-shirts will be awarded to the next subsequent winners who have not won a T-shirt previously.

The top 10 winners in each Sonic World Ranking Contest will accrue World Ranking Points as follows: 1st place = 5 points; 2nd place = 4 points; 3rd place = 3 points; 4th place through 10th place = 1 point.

At the completion of the Sonic World Ranking contests, the points will be totaled and the top 10 point earners will receive a complete set of Sonic Adventure plush characters (Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, Big, Amy and Chao) and a custom-made Sonic Jacket.

Additionally, the player who has collected all 130 emblems found in the Sonic game in the fastest time will be awarded a special trophy separate from the World Rankings trophy.

Approximate value of all prizes is $4,000.00 (US)

3. HOW TO PARTICIPATE: To enter, use your Sega Dreamcast console and Sonic Adventure game to play E-102 Gamma in Final Egg. Once your game is over, save your game, and upload your saved file to the Sonic Adventure Homepage. You can also submit comments with your game. You can upload as many times as you would like and Sega will record your best [time/score/fish weight]; however, all entries must be submitted using the same email address. Sega is not responsible for game entries that are late, lost, incomplete or misdirected due to transmission and/or computer error. Game entries become the property of Sega and will not be returned.
4. CONTEST DURATION: The new rules governing Sonic World Ranking contests will take affect starting with the 5th World Rankings contest which is schedule to open on June 16, 2000. Sonic World Rankings contests are currently scheduled through June 30, 2000. Contest schedule and rules are subject to change.

5. ODDS OF WINNING: Odds of winning depend on the number of entries received, which is anticipated to be approximately 1:1,000.
6. SELECTION OF WINNERS: Sega will rank all eligible game entries on the basis of [time/score/weight of fish]. For each World Rankings contest, prizes will be awarded to the top 10 entrants. At the end of the World Rankings contests, Sega will rank all players World Ranking Points and will rank the times of all players collecting all 130 emblems. Prizes will be awarded to the top 10 point earners and to the player who has collected all 130 emblems in the fastest time. Winners will be notified via email and/or telephone call. Prizes not claimed within 30 days of notification will be forfeited and awarded to an alternate winner. Prizes won by minors may be awarded in the name of a parent or legal guardian. Allow 4-8 weeks for delivery of prizes.
