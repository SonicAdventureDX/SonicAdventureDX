BREEDING AND EGG CARRIER CHAO
I havent had chao for long and was wondering how to breed them. Also, in the prison on the egg carrier I found an egg, but I couldnt take it to the garden!!! Help me!!!

Greg

Greg, when you rescue the egg in Egg Carrier (that sounds funny), take it to a door all the way to the end. There, you will find a transporter that will take you to the Egg Carrier Chao Garden. You now have an exotic Black Chao! For breeding, when your Chao is ready, it will sit down and a bed of flowers will sprout around it. Place a mature Chao in the flowerbed and a new Chao Egg will appear! You can also marry two Chao via the VMU! Happy parenting!

VMU SPACE: THE FINAL FRONTIER
so i got a chao on my vmu, and it's taking up some 150 blocks. now i dont want to delete my chao but i need more memory, and the chao's 150 some blocks are still taking up space after i dropped him off in the chao gardens. can i get rid of all those filled blocks without deleting my chao?

Andrew

Andrew, to make space, take your Chao out of the VMU and into the Chao Garden, then go to File Options (X+Y+A+B until you get to the menu) and delete the 128 block Chao file. You can also delete the 11 block Upload file if you are finished with it. Voila! More room on your VMU!

FROZEN VMU FROM CHAO BREEDING
when i downloaded a chao from daycare. and tried breeding using vmu connection. the other vmu is now stuck on "keep connect." The vmu is now "frozen." The other vmu with the downloaded chao data still says "CHAO Download Data." What did I do wrong?

Bruce & Jennifer Lord

Before you can marry Chao that have been downloaded from Daycare, first you need to place them in your Chao Garden to recouperate from the trip through the internet! After a short time (give it a minute or two), you can put that Chao in a VMU and then it can be ready to mate. HP... IT'S NOT HEWLETT PACKARD IS IT?
what does hp mean in the vmu?

William Scott

HP means Hit Points. In Chao Adventure, you need to keep your Chao's stats above zero so he can survive. If your Chao's HP reaches Zero, it is toast.
