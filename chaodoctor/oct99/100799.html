SLEEPY, SLEEPY CHAO
How do I keep my chao from being sleepy all the time. Its pretty annoying when I want to race them. Please help.

Nick Silva

Nick, you gotta give your Chao some rest. If you are seeing your Chao sleep during a race, it's a good indication that it's not well rested. In the Garden, if you see your Chao yawn or act sleepy, give it a Chaonut or two! Wait until after it has awakened before you try and race it. That should improve it's performance!
