VMU LOCK-UP
When I was breeding two chaos in VMUs, one of the VMUs locked up and now just displays "Keep Connect." How do I "unlock" it to get my chao back? I hope I didn't lose it!

Aaron Karp

Aaron, what an unfortunate incident! However, there is a way to recover. Take the pointy side of a pen (or something similar) and push the RESET button on the back of the VMU (it's in a small hole on the left side of the battery cover). That should unlock your VMU and you will not lose any data!

RESCUED ANIMALS
how do you introduce animals you ruscue to your chao?

Caotic

Ah, back to Chao 101. Take a character (Sonic or anyone else) and go through an action stage. When you defeat any of Doctor Robotnik's creatures, an animal will be saved from it. Pick up the animal and you will see it pop up on the bottom right of your screen. After you clear the level, go into the Chao Garden and you will see all the collected animals released into the garden! Please note that you cannot do this if you selected TRIAL as your game mode.

SUMO CHAO
I've heard rumours of a sumo chao. Is this true, and if so, how do I get him?

G. Miller

Ah, the Sumo Chao! Here's a hint... pay attention... Chao evolve depending on what animals it is given. Certain animals share characteristics (i.e., swimming, running, etc..). If you give a Chao a certain characteristic enough times, it will evolve to a shape that can better take advantage of that characteristic (i.e., Water Chao, Sonic Chao, etc.). After a Chao's first evolution, you can give it a different characteristic and it will eventually evolve again! The key to a Sumo Chao? Swimming > Power. Good luck!

HUGGING GOOD NiGHTS
Where on earth do you find the NiGHTS chao? I've searched for ages and ages but to no avail, please help! Also, could you tell me how to hug/cuddle a chao?

mpugh1

A NiGHTS Chao is bred, not found! I hear purple is it's favorite color. If you want to hug a Chao, just pick it up with the Y button, and keep the button pressed! Then, move the analog stick in any direction to hug it! To put the Chao down, just let go of the analog stick and press Y.
